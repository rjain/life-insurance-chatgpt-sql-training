# Business Interpretation Prompts

## Executive Summary

```text
Summarize the analysis for a life insurance operations leader.

Use plain business language.
Include:
1. What changed or stands out
2. Why it matters
3. Which segments need attention
4. Data quality caveats
5. Recommended next actions
```

## Analyst Review

```text
Review the result as a skeptical data analyst.

Call out:
1. Metric definition risks
2. Join risks
3. Selection bias
4. Small sample sizes
5. Data quality issues
6. What should be validated before sharing with executives
```
